About Me

Hello! I'm Stephanie, a trainer and educator specializing in educational technology. I'm currently open for work!

My background is primarily in teaching English as a Second language. You can see my teaching philosophy and teacher training presentations included in this portfolio. I also have a background in software engineering, and my current goal is to merge my passions for technology and education to work in the field of corporate training. My research interests include educational technology, tech literacy, and adult learning. When I'm not at work, I like to play guitar, paint watercolors, and code!
