About Me

Hello! I'm Stephanie, a Global Teaching Fellow at Tokyo International University in Japan. Right now, I teach first year listening and speaking classes for our Japanese students and IT literacy for our international students. I've also taught Academic Composition and EFL reading and writing.

I love the challenge of helping students to have fun and use authentic language in the classroom. My research interests include encouraging L2 use in the classroom, using AR (augmented reality) for different classroom activities, and teaching tech to our students. When I'm not at work, I like to improve my Japanese skills, check out different hikes around Japan, travel, and sing karaoke.
