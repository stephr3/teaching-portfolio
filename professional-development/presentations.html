"Using Augmented Reality with HP Reveal"
Presenters: Adam Garnica and Stephanie Roth
Presented to GTI Staff November 21, 2018

Have you ever played Pokemon Go or tried a virtual makeup app? If so, you've enjoyed the benefits of augmented reality. Augmented reality (AR) is an exciting new technology that is easily accessible to any student who owns a smartphone. So, how can you incorporate AR in your classroom? Join Stephanie and Adam for a practical workshop on augmented reality using HP Reveal. By the end of the workshop, you should have a little bit of theory, some new classroom activity ideas, and your very own AR video added to your teaching toolkit. The future is now - use AR in your class today!
